Our Agenda

1
Scale It Down
Standardization before optimisation. We need a talented, dedicated group of musicians to maintain a profesional orchestra.
2
Cut The Bull
Let's be honest. Conyngham is not doing the MGSSO any favours. Cut the bull, and bring back the good old days of Harry Potter and Lord of the Ring.
3
Bring Back The Solos
Y11 solos are the foundation of a strong Y12 solo programme. We can only play amazing repetoire if our solists have had prior experience with the orchestra.
